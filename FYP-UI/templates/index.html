Welcome to Spoken Tutorial!

Promoting IT literacy through videos

Spoken Tutorial is an initiative to help students across the country learn about various free and open source software through audio-video tutorials. The students can refer from various forums that discuss the content being taught in the videos through the links that are provided while the student is watching the video. This helps the student in gathering more information in the topic he/she is learning.
